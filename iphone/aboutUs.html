The customer is

A small team of five striving to create innovative yet useful web products that always places customers as the heart and soul of these solutions.

NOICE is our first initiative that takes a go at making the online and physical shopping experience a walk in the park for any customer. The project is funded and supported by WooksMart, a Sri Lankan retail company and supermarket.

WooksMart shares our objective to always keep the customers at the top and NOICE aims to do just that.
